The Man Behind the Music

MC Curtis is an up and coming independent artist from the Canada who blends his lyrical ability with his soft melodies to deliver his unique style. His style is centered around Hip-Hop but influences of Pop, Pop Punk along with R&B can be felt in his music as well.MC Curtis started in the music industry when he created his distribution and management company Underground Comeup Records (UCU). In early 2019 he released a collaborative album with artists from Toronto to showcase there talent called "Toronto After Dark" (MC Curtis did not actually rap on this project). In July 2019 to follow up, he released "Toronto After Dark 2" which had the first appearance of MC Curtis with his first ever release "Fuck With the Vision" which has received over 20 thousands streams on Spotify which significantly out performed the rest of the tracks on Toronto After Dark 2. MC Curtis has been dedicated to his craft working hard for over 2 years now and has an arsenal of unreleased music covering all styles and moods waiting to be released! He looks to keep the momentum he gained in early 2021 from Too Lit and Routine rolling into late 2021 and early 2022! Make sure to stay tuned and get ready to see a career unfold in front of your eyes!
