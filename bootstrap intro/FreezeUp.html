MC Curtis

Freeze Up

"Freeze Up" is the first single following up MC Curtis' first ever mixtape "The Intro". Contrary to his mixtape release, "Freeze Up" was recorded at an infamous studio in Toronto called "Majur Musik Studios" which have had many notable toronto artists record there including Killy, Top5, 3MFrench, WhyG, and Booggz to name a few. At the studio, Curtis worked with and recorded the track along side and with help from engineer pop kris (who has credits with famous artists such as Cardi B and Popcaan). This song has a more relaxed laid-back vibe and following some success from "The Intro", MC Curtis felt like this is a great song to follow up with to continue to show his diversity in his art and show people what he has to offer!
