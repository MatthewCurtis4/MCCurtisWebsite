The Man Behind the Music

MC Curtis is an up and coming rapper from the Greater Toronto Area. MC Curtis started in the music industry when he created his distribution and management company Underground Comeup Records (UCU). In early 2019 he released a collaborative album with artists from Toronto to showcase there talent called "Toronto After Dark" (MC Curtis did not actually rap on this project). In July 2019 to follow up, he released "Toronto After Dark 2" which had the first appearance of MC Curtis with his first ever release "Fuck With the Vision" which has received over 20 thousands streams on spotify which significantly out performed the rest of the tracks on Toronto After Dark 2. MC Curtis is currently in university for software engineering so his schedule is pretty chaotic but he still finds time to dedicate to his craft. MC Curtis has dropped a couple of songs all leading up to his first EP release titled "The Intro" which he released June 1st which has already garnered thousands of streams! This tape included standout tracks such as Freestyle Henny Which already has surpassed 10k streams on soundcloud alone and No Cap which is currently sitting at over 7k streams on Spotify. Make sure to stay up to date and follow me on my journey and check out my new single Freeze Up, you won't be disappointed!
