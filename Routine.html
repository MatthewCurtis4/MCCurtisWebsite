MC Curtis

Routine

Newest Release

MC Curtis is an up and coming rapper from the Toronto area with a passion to create. "Routine" is MC Curtis' follow up after a short lull from releases following single "Too Lit" which was was recorded at the established and well known studio in Toronto called "Majur Musik Studios" which have had many notable Toronto artists record there including Killy, Top5, 3MFrench, WhyG, and Booggz to name a few. At the studio, Curtis worked with and recorded the track along side and with help from engineer Pop Kris (who has credits with famous artists such as Cardi B and Popcaan). This song has a more high energy flexing energy and MC Curtis felt like this is a grgieat song to follow up with to continue to show his diversity in his art and show people what he has to offer!
